Q & A

What is “Crypto Trade Bot” ?

Crypto Trade Bot is an algorithmic trading engine that trades automatically crypto-currencies on multiple exchanges.

How does it work ?

Crypto Trade Bot processes historical data and makes buy or sell decisions based on actual price trend, currently held positions and former trades using a preselected strategy.

What should I provide to Crypto Trade Bot ?

Crypto Trade Bot only trades on your account. You need to provide your exchange API keys but you’ll always stay the owner of your assets.

What are the advantages ?

Crypto Trade Bot can trade multiple crypto-currencies at the same time and makes decision based purely of factual data.

Will it earn money ?

One cannot predict the outcome of the crypto market, the default strategy needs a bullish trend in order to generate gains.

Is it risky ?

There are sanity checks in the system preventing abnormal sell/buy protecting users from "fat finger" trades :

  • The system never sells “at market” but always “at price limit” : meaning that it will only sell at a known value but also that there is no guarantee that the order issued will be ever executed.
  • The system doesn’t issue order to the exchange when the price coming from the historical data diverge from the current price by more than 5%. Making sure that that decision is based on data that are close to current market conditions.

How much does it cost ?

Crypto Trade Bot is free to use.

How can I use it ?

User needs to provide the API keys of the exchange to use. These keys allows the bot to issue trade order and to perform balance calculation on behalf of the user. The keys should be configured not to allow withdrawal. Therefore they could not be used to withdraw from the exchange without users consents. User can remove the key from the service at any time.
